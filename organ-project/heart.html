The Structure of the Heart

The human heart is a four-chambered organ that works as a pump to circulate blood throughout the body. Its walls are made up of cardiac muscle.

Heart Function

The heart pumps oxygen-rich blood to the body and returns oxygen-poor blood to the lungs. This circulation is essential for life.
